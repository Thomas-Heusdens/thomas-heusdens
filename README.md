# 💫 About Me:
I’m Thomas Heusdens, a graduated Multimedia & Creative Technologies student currently in my second year of a new bachelor’s degree in Applied Computer Science at Erasmushogeschool Brussel in Belgium.<br>I’m passionate about software development and especially love the backend world — building logic, structuring architectures, and creating scalable systems. But I also enjoy switching to the frontend, because nothing feels better than visualizing the logic I’ve built and turning it into an actual experience.<br><br>Over the years, I’ve explored multiple stacks and technologies. My first full-stack website was built in MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), a stack I still love to use. I’ve also developed projects in Laravel and Spring Boot, both following MVC patterns in their own unique way, and both frameworks that I still enjoy working with.<br>I’m a big fan of Java, JavaScript, and TypeScript, and I especially love using Next.js to build fast, modern, SEO-optimised websites supported by strong backend logic.<br><br>I code in many other programming languages as well, which you’ll find in the next part of this README.<br><br>Outside of coding, I’m 22 years old and living in Dilbeek, a small city just west of Brussels. I’m also very passionate about sports, whether it’s football (soccer), padel, the gym, or running, staying active is a big part of who I am.
